Overview

A bus for the whole city

The Arrival Bus was developed to meet global cities’ needs for sustainability, affordability, and equity in public transport. It offers flexible passenger seating across a flat floor — more comfort and standing space — with wrap-around exterior and interior screens, adaptable lighting, a transparent roof, and a suite of digital features.

My work

Sound and touch

I worked on the AVAS (Acoustic Vehicle Alerting System) sound and on interior touchpoints, including the bell push and USB.

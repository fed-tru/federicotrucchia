Overview

Waste in, fertiliser out

Tabemaru is a patented composter that leverages the ability of Black Soldier Flies to quickly turn food waste into frass. The frass is a valuable resource — it can be used as organic fertiliser, which is pivotal to enabling organic farming.

My role

Strategy, funding, prototypes

As Director I defined the strategy, secured long-term funding, and built work-like / look-like prototypes.

Overview

Driving arrays of electromagnets

The Mag-Operator is an electronic platform designed to let makers and artists easily interact with large arrays of electromagnets.

How it works

An Arduino shield and 64 magnets

The system pairs an Arduino shield — carrying all the components needed for control — with a support PCB holding a matrix of 64 electromagnets. By switching individual magnets on and off, you can move any magnetic material across the surface and generate organised patterns.

Origin

From ferrofluid art to a tool

It began as an attempt to build an art installation where floating drops of ferrofluid were controlled magnetically. I soon realised that kind of interactive installation gets extremely complex on the electronics side — so the focus shifted from the installation itself to a tool that hides that complexity, letting anyone concentrate on the interaction they want rather than the wiring behind it.

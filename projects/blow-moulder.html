Project 15

DIY Blow-moulder

A home-built machine that melts plastic bottles with heat and compressed air to give them a second life as something else.

Overview

A second life for plastic bottles

I set out to build a machine that could melt and reshape plastic bottles with compressed air — giving them a second life as a different object.

How it works

Heat, spin, inject

The bottle is secured to a universal lid that also allows a rotational movement — needed to heat the plastic uniformly — driven by a stepper motor. Once the plastic is malleable, the mould closes and high-pressure air from a compressor is injected to form the new shape.

How it went

Some result, some danger

I achieved some kind of result — and also very nearly took myself out with a valve malfunction and a bit of high voltage. To the next one!

Overview

A lamp from a single sheet

01Lamp is a desktop LED lamp with a body made from a single sheet of corrugated cardboard. It was designed to be distributed two ways at once: as a commercial product, and as an open-source set of instructions.

The industrial version is produced with the fewest possible processes — the body is die-cut from one printed sheet, and the light comes from a standard LED strip. The DIY version is an online tutorial that guides the maker through folding and locking the shape, origami-like. Any large pizza box will do.

Origin

From a 3D trick to a company

The project started as an exploration of an unwrapping feature in 3D software — a way to design and iterate complex cardboard shapes quickly. Once I found the aesthetic, 01Lamp was meant to be released only as an online tutorial.

Talking with designer friends, we decided to form a team and grow the project. From a simple tutorial we started focusing on circularity, and on how to turn discarded materials into new products. We prepared a small production run for the 2012 Fuorisalone in Milan; the response was strong enough that we tried crowdfunding — and Fattelo! became the first Italian startup funded that way.

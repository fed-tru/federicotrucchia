Overview

Follow the lights

LUMI is a fully illuminated keyboard that pairs with the LUMI app. Players choose a song, follow the lights on LUMI Keys, and start playing immediately — learning music as they go. As the first fully modular portable keyboard, LUMI Keys can double or triple in size by magnetically snapping multiple keyboards together.

The build

The most ambitious hardware hack I’ve built

To test the experience before the real product existed, I produced a high-fidelity prototype in under a month — by reverse-engineering a competitor keyboard and rebuilding it around the BLOCKS ecosystem.

I disassembled it, chopped the PCB, and connected its sensor lines to the board of a Seaboard Block PCB. With a firmware engineer, we learned to intercept and map the MIDI signals coming from the donor keyboard and disguise them as perfect-pitch signals from a Seaboard. Those messages were also routed to an Arduino driving the LEDs on a custom-designed PCB.

Finally, I built a bottom chassis by welding together two Seaboard Block chassis, 3D-modelled and printed the top structures, and machined and laser-cut the keys to maximise light diffusion. The result let a test group interact with LUMI on Noise — our mobile app — alongside the rest of the BLOCKS instruments.

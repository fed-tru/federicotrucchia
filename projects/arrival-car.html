Overview

Built for the people who drive it all day

The Arrival Car is designed for the ride-hailing industry, offering an excellent experience for drivers and passengers while promoting clean urban air. It provides exceptional visibility and comfort, with legroom about twice as large as that of an average car of the same length.

My work

Strategy and the pack

I worked on commercialisation strategy and HV battery-pack design, and acted as OKR master for the program — keeping objectives and key results aligned across a fast-moving team.

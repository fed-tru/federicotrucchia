Overview

A lamp from a Tetrapak brick

02 Noctambula is a switch-operated, battery-powered LED lamp, designed — like 01Lamp before it — to be available both as a finished product and as a free online tutorial.

The build

The body is the circuit

Co-designed with Mireia Gordi Vila, the tutorial teaches makers to turn a Tetrapak brick into a functional lamp, using the aluminium inside the carton as part of the electric circuit.

The commercial version is made entirely with digital manufacturing: a coloured-cardboard body cut on any laser cutter, and a conductive circuit of copper tape cut on a plotter. Because the body and the electronics are the same part, it took several iterations to balance a sturdy design with a circuit that actually works.

About

My career runs across the full spectrum of innovation and product development — academia, startups, a unicorn, and design studios.

The work is consistent even when the products aren’t: interaction systems built from light, sound, touch and behaviour, prototyped until they actually work. This portfolio is a living record — for myself, and for anyone deciding whether to work with me.
